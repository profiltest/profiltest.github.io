Who we are

Progetazzione
Architettura +
Natura

PAN associati S.R.L. is a company founded in 2001 made up of a multidisciplinary group of professionals who operate in the fields of architectural and landscape design, up to territorial planning.

Our mission is to revitalize degraded spaces, converting them into livable and sustainable areas that are of value to local communities.

We see these spaces as an opportunity to improve urban life, create new habitats and ecosystems, foster biodiversity, and bring ecological benefits through infrastructure that provides ecosystem services. We are committed to the fight against climate change by adopting design strategies inspired by nature-based solutions (NBS).

What do we do

Our areas of expertise

Through a multidisciplinary approach, our activity extends to both the public and private sectors, offering a range of services that covers all phases of design, construction management and safety coordination, both during the design and execution phases.

We also offer specialist consultancy in the environmental and landscape sector, including feasibility studies, agronomic, botanical and forestry analyses, urban planning analyzes and studies, checks and environmental studies for the authorization of plans and projects.

In our creative process, we pay attention to every aspect of the local ecosystem, aiming to develop functional and resilient landscapes.
